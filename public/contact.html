Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How can I submit my own recipe to Mesmeric Taste Lexicon? +

We welcome recipe submissions from talented home cooks and professional chefs. Please email your recipe in a structured format (ingredients, instructions, cooking time, difficulty level) along with high-quality photos to submissions@mesmerictaste.com. Our editorial team reviews all submissions and will contact you within 2-3 weeks if your recipe is selected for publication.

Can I use your recipes for my cooking class or restaurant menu? +

Our recipes are protected by copyright, but we offer licensing options for educational and commercial use. For cooking classes, we provide special educator licenses. Restaurants interested in featuring our recipes should contact our partnerships team at partners@mesmerictaste.com to discuss licensing agreements and potential collaborations.

Do you offer cooking classes or workshops? +

Yes! We host monthly virtual cooking workshops and occasional in-person masterclasses at our San Francisco studio. Our workshops range from beginner techniques to advanced culinary skills. Subscribe to our newsletter to receive notifications about upcoming classes and early registration opportunities.

How accurate are the nutritional information and cooking times? +

All our recipes are extensively tested in our culinary studio. Cooking times are based on standard kitchen equipment and may vary. Nutritional information is calculated using industry-standard software but should be considered estimates. For specific dietary requirements, we recommend consulting with a nutrition professional.

Can I request a specific type of recipe or cuisine? +

Absolutely! We value community input for our recipe development. While we can't guarantee all requests will be fulfilled, we prioritize the most requested themes and cuisines. Submit your recipe requests through our contact form with the subject "Recipe Request" or join our community forum to vote on upcoming recipe categories.
