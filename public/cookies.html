Cookies Policy

Learn how Mesmeric Taste Lexicon uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize content, and analyze website traffic. Last updated: November 2023.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the site owners.

At Mesmeric Taste Lexicon, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors interact with our content. This helps us improve our recipes, cooking guides, and overall website functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies on our website, each serving a different purpose:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Necessary for website functionality, user authentication, and security Session or up to 24 months
Functional Remember your preferences (language, region, dietary filters) Up to 12 months
Analytics Understand how visitors use our site to improve content and features Up to 24 months
Advertising Deliver relevant culinary content and recipe recommendations Up to 12 months

Detailed Cookie Information

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as logging in or filling out forms. You can set your browser to block these cookies, but some parts of the site may not work.

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser, used to maintain your session.
  • Security Cookies: Help protect against malicious activity and unauthorized access.
  • Authentication Cookies: Remember your login status and preferences.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language, region, dietary preferences, and cooking skill level.
  • Recipe Saver Cookies: Remember recipes you've saved to your "Favorites" or "Cook Later" lists.
  • Shopping List Cookies: Remember ingredients you've added to your shopping list.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us know which recipes are most popular and how visitors move around the site.

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and provides information about visitor behavior.
  • Heatmap Analytics: Understand where users click and scroll on recipe pages.
  • Conversion Tracking: Measure the effectiveness of our cooking guides and tutorials.

Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites.

  • Retargeting Cookies: Show you recipes or cooking equipment you viewed earlier.
  • Social Media Cookies: Enable you to share recipes on social platforms.
  • Affiliate Cookies: Track referrals to cooking equipment or ingredient suppliers.

Manage Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

Browser Controls for Cookies

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

Popular Browser Settings:

  1. Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  2. Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  3. Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  4. Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and data stored

Note: Blocking all cookies may impact your experience on our website, as some features may not work properly without cookies. Essential cooking functions like saving recipes, creating shopping lists, and personalized recommendations require cookies to function correctly.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data use practices. We encourage you to check this page periodically for the latest information on our cookie practices.
